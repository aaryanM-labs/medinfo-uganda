Save these numbers now

Do not wait until an emergency to look for these numbers. Save 999, 112, and the Ministry of Health hotline 0800 100 066 in your phone contacts right now. In an emergency, every second counts.

Contact information is provided in good faith and may change. Always verify numbers with official sources. In any life-threatening situation call 999 or 112 immediately.
